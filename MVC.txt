MVC-> Model,View,Controller
      project kain ander likhne kaa ek tarika hota hain.

Models-> iska kaam hota hain database kain models ko store karana.
Views-> iska kaam hota hain hamare frontend ko store karana.
Controllers-> iske ander hum apne backend ko store karate hain.

MVC design pattern sain hum apne code ko aur compact bana sakte hain.

router.route-> isko use karne sain hum common routes ko sirf ek baar hi likhne ki jaroorat padegi.
